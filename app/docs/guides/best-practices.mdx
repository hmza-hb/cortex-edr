# Security Best Practices

When securing your deployments with CortexEDR, maintaining these best practices drastically lowers your risk footprint.

## 1. Zero-Trust Architecture
Assume every component is vulnerable until proven otherwise.

## 2. Shift-Left Mentality
Integrate CortexEDR early in your CI/CD pipelines rather than auditing finished releases.

## 3. Limit Secrets
Avoid committing tokens or keys; our scanning agents prioritize exposing leaked credentials.
